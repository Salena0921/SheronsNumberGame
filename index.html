Shenron's Number Guess

Will Shenron grant your wish?
Instructions: Shenron has decided to grant a wish to those who can use the Dragonballs to match his number. What you will have to do to gain that wish is to get your numbers to add up to Shenrons. Each dragonball has a random number attached to it. Click the Dragonball to add that number to your total guess. If you number matches Shenron's then you gain 1 power up and if you gain 7 power ups you will win your wish. However, if your number is greater then Shenron's then you lose and will have to start over at the beginning. So, be careful and good luck because if you get past Shenron you will enter Porunga's game. Once you hit 7 Power ups Sheron will appear. Click on Sheron to continue to Porunga's game. Be very careful because some of Porunga's dragonballs will set you back in your count.
